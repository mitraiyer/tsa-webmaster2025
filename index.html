Our Roots

At Noorani, our name, in itself, carries the essence of our culinary palette. When breaking up our name, ‘Noor’ – means light, symbolizing the warmth and rich heritage that Indian and Middle Eastern bring to our table. Our exquisitely picked-out menu shines with bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and traditional food, bringing together two cultures and creating culinary excellence.
