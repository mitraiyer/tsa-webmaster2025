Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for lunch and dinner. You can make a reservation by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly at (123) 456-7890, or using our online reservation system on our website.

What cuisine does Noorani serve?

Noorani specializes in a fusion of Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines. Our menu features traditional dishes from both regions with our unique culinary twist, using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We offer catering for events of all sizes, from intimate gatherings to large celebrations. Our catering menu can be customized to meet your specific needs and preferences. Please contact us for more information about our catering services and pricing.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We have a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available. If you have specific dietary restrictions or allergies, please let us know when making your reservation or placing your order, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate your needs.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot available for our guests. Additionally, there is street parking available in the surrounding area. Valet parking is available Friday and Saturday evenings.
